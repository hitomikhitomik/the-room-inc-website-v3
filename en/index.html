About The Room Inc.

The Room Inc. specializes in the comprehensive management of meticulously selected luxury vacation rental properties across Japan. We provide end-to-end solutions to help property owners maximize their investment potential and offer guests an unforgettable stay. From land sourcing and design to operations and marketing, we support our clients at every stage to ensure optimal returns and guest satisfaction.

Our expertise and attention to detail transform your property into a true sanctuary. We blend authentic Japanese hospitality with international standards to create a unique and unparalleled luxury experience.
