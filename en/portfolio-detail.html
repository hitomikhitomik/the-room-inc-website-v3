Property Overview

"Kyoto Hidden Villa" is a luxury vacation rental located in a quiet residential area of Higashiyama-ku, Kyoto, combining the charm of a traditional Kyoto machiya with modern comforts. The spacious living room overlooking a beautiful Japanese garden, a kitchen equipped with the latest amenities, and bedrooms with high-quality bedding provide guests with ultimate relaxation and tranquility.

Major tourist attractions suchs as Kiyomizu-dera Temple, Gion, and Yasaka Shrine are within walking distance, allowing guests to fully experience Kyoto's history and culture. The private garden, where you can feel Japan's four seasons, is a special place that makes you forget the hustle and bustle of the city.

The expert team at The Room Inc. meticulously manages the operations of this property, maintaining high occupancy rates and guest satisfaction. With attention to detail and personalized service based on the spirit of Japanese "Omotenashi," we deliver the best experience to both owners and guests.
