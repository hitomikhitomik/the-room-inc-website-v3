Our Services

Land Sourcing & Design

The Room Inc. provides comprehensive support from selecting ideal land that aligns with your vision and investment goals, to the design and development of the property. We collaborate with experienced architects and designers to create properties that blend seamlessly with Japan's rich nature and culture while meeting modern luxury standards.

We consider local regulations, scenery, accessibility, and future market value to identify the optimal land. In the design phase, we merge traditional Japanese aesthetics with cutting-edge comfort to create a unique space. We also prioritize sustainability and efficiency, proposing designs that maximize long-term value.

Operations & Management

Entrust the daily operations and management of your property to The Room Inc.'s expert team. We provide a seamless and personalized experience for guests from check-in to check-out. We handle all tasks necessary to maintain the property in its best condition, including cleaning, maintenance, security, and replenishment of consumables.

We also manage emergency responses, regular inspections, and planning and execution of repairs, preserving the property's value in the long term. Property owners can operate their properties with peace of mind, free from the burden of daily tasks.

Marketing & Sales

We implement targeted marketing strategies to ensure your property reaches affluent domestic and international guests. This includes high-resolution photography, professional property descriptions, and promotion through major luxury travel booking sites and our exclusive network.

We maximize the unique appeal of your property and communicate it as a compelling story. We also optimize pricing, manage bookings, and handle customer service to maximize occupancy and revenue. Based on data analysis, we constantly monitor market trends and adjust our strategies accordingly.

Enhancing Guest Experience

The Room Inc. focuses on providing not just accommodation, but an unforgettable experience. From before arrival, throughout their stay, and after departure, we support guests in creating lasting memories with meticulous care and personalized service.

We arrange local cultural experiences, private chefs, transportation, and cater to all guest needs. Based on the spirit of Japanese "Omotenashi" (hospitality), we provide service with attention to every detail, leading to repeat guests and high ratings.
